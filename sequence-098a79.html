sequence

a step sequencer with a chord-relative harmonic engine. set a root and a scale, author chords by degree — the rest of the band harmonizes against whatever played last.

you have this link because chris sent it to you. it isn't published anywhere else — treat it accordingly.

download for macos
apple silicon · auto-updates itself

install: open the dmg, drag sequence to applications.

first run: macos will ask for microphone access — that's how the app enumerates audio devices; say yes.

samples: the app reads kits from ~/documents/sequence/samples, organized as drums/ bass/ instruments/ pads/ textures/ breaks/ loops/ — one folder of wavs per kit. it starts empty; a starter pack is coming.

updates arrive in-app — it checks on launch and asks before installing.
