sequence

hello friends, welcome to my madness. as you know from listening to me blather on for the last couple months, i've been building this software based sequencer. this app is the culmination of that effort and i'd love your thoughts and ideas. there is A LOT of functionality in here and zero documentation (yay!) so please just hit me up with any questions or concerns.

everything audio related is based on samples. fastest start: download my whole sample library as a zip (heads up: 1.1 gb), unzip it wherever you want, and point the app at that folder (see notes below). individual kits are also available here, and the app can send midi out if you'd rather control your hardware.

since we live in the age of social media if you enjoy this and wanna talk about it online please tag in @newspeechsound on instagram so that my clout and internet credibility will skyrocket <3

download for macos
apple silicon + intel · auto-updates itself

install: open the dmg, drag sequence to applications.

first run: macos will ask for microphone access — that's how the app enumerates audio devices; say yes.

samples: easiest path — download the full sample library (1.1 gb zip), unzip it anywhere, then in the app: settings (gear) → user samples → change… → pick that folder. it rescans and every kit shows up.

or build your own: the app reads kits from ~/documents/sequence/samples by default, organized as drums/ bass/ instruments/ pads/ textures/ breaks/ loops/ — one folder of wavs per kit. grab individual kits from the sample library and unzip each into its category folder (e.g. drums/blck_noir/).

updates arrive in-app — it checks on launch and asks before installing.
