hello there

this experiment has been a long time coming. it's interesting to see how priorities, deadlines, expectations and requirements shift goals and ideas over the course of a decade or so. i've always been interested in the collision of technology and music - i'm pretty sure the only reason i was included in the founding of the armed was because i self-recorded demos that sounded better than anyone else in our circles.

maybe this is a return to that initial thinking - i always loved creating and releasing things for free, removing the commerce side of music and focusing on the parts that excited me.

## what this is

the core idea here is that everything that comes out of this new project will live under this umbrella, this for now is a website with a couple toys on it, but as the project grows all of the tools, music, debris and concepts will live here and hopefully start to tell a story of some kind. my aim is to produce and share as many aspects of this music process as possible.
